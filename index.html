Welcome to the Minecraft mob exhibit! In this exhibit you can explore the plethora of mobs Minecraft has to offer!
Below is a tree, where you can find the mobs of Minecraft. Click on their names to view and compare their health points and damage in the scatter plot below. The size of the circles representing the mobs scales to their health points, so the bigger the healthier! The stars of the mobs represent how much damage they can deal, and it also scales. The larger the star the deadlier! Hover over a mob's name for a tooltip with more information.
